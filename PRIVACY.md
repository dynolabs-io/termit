# Termit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Summary

Termit collects nothing. No accounts. No analytics. No telemetry. No tracking.

## Details

### Data we collect
None.

### Data we store on your device
- Hostnames, usernames, and tags you create for SSH/Mosh hosts.
- SSH private keys generated by Termit. These live in the iOS Secure Enclave and never leave your device.
- Optional snippets, themes, and preferences.

### Data we transmit
- SSH and Mosh protocol traffic, peer-to-peer between your iPhone and the server you connect to. We are not in the path.
- Optional iCloud sync of hosts/snippets — encrypted with a symmetric key stored only on your device. Apple sees ciphertext.

### Third-party services
- Apple iCloud (only if you enable sync).
- That's it.

### Crash reports
Termit does not collect crash reports. If iOS prompts you to share crash reports with developers, those go to Apple, not to us, and we do not receive them.

### Data deletion
Uninstall the app. All data is removed.

### Source code
Termit is open source under MIT: https://github.com/dynolabs-io/termit. You can verify everything in this policy by reading the code.
